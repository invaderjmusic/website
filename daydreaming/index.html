Daydreaming

My first "proper" release, Daydreaming had two songs, both with vocals. Two of my producer friends, Feàrn and Harris Hall, produced a song each. Their help was indispensable in the creation of this single.

Listen Now View Lyrics

Is It Over?

This song is about the realisation that life has moved on. The time for you to be with the one you love is over. I wrote this song to come to terms with the fact that we will never be together.

This song was produced by Harris Hall, a friend of mine. Using his expertise in electronic production, he created the song's synthwave sound with 80s synths and reverbed drums. I couldn't have done it without him.

Notes from Harris
“My greatest challenge during the production of Is It Over was probably troubleshooting the mixing and trying to get the vocals to be heard well. Feàrn (the producer on the other track) actually gave me some advice on this.”

Daydreaming

My thoughts kept returning to someone special. No matter what I was doing, or what I was thinking of, I really couldn't get them off my mind. That's what this song's about - the rest of the lyrics are filler.

I found it quite hard to record the vocals for this one. That's a lesson learnt for next time – I need to make sure I can sing the part while I'm writing it. So, I cobbled together my best shot, combining lines from different good takes.

This song was produced by Feàrn, a friend of mine. With on-point production, he significantly improved the song with some backing vocals he threw in for fun. I couldn't have done it without him.

Notes from Feàrn
“The biggest challenge in producing Daydreaming was getting the parts lined up properly during the rall. at the end of the track.”
