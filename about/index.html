Hi, I'm InVadeRJ. I live in Scotland today but I'm from many places around the world.

I can't seem to write a song that isn't about heartbreak. Though in a variety of genres, my songs share a signature melancholic tone with French lyrics, recorder parts and minor 4 chords sprinkled in to the mix.

My musical journey began when my mother taught me to play the recorder and the piano as a child. I joined choirs during middle and high school, solidifying my love for singing. Despite this, I never manage to sing my own songs as well as they sound in my head...

My existing interest in composition only became a passion for songwriting in 2021. Armed with free music software including MuseScore and LMMS, I created and released my first song - I Loved You - in 2022. I had written lyrics but was too embarrassed to record the vocal part, so the track released as an instrumental.
